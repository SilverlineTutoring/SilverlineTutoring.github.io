FAQs


What are the qualifications of the tutors?

A lot of the tutors are in college or are seniors in high school that excel at the subject they teach

How do I sign up to be a student?

Fill out the Google Form for students that can be found here

How do I sign up to be a tutor?

Fill out the Google Form for tutors that can be found here

Is the tutoring free?

Yes!!

Is the tutoring online or in person?

Most tutoring sessions are conducted online via Zoom, Discord, or some other calling platform due to the ongoing pandemic, but this is completely up to the decisions of the tutor and student

Can anyone sign up for tutoring?

Yes, it is free to anyone of all ages

How long does it take to get a tutor?

You should be assigned a tutor and be contacted by your tutor withing seven days of filling out the student form

How often can a student receive tutoring?

You and the student set up tutoring based of both of your schedules. There is no set limit to the amount of tutoring one can receive
