Welcome to Silverline Tutoring

Who We Are

Silverline Tutoring is a non-profit organization that offers high school students one-on-one communication with other high school students on various subjects. We believe that learning should be a service for students by students. Our approach to tutoring helps both the tutor and the tutee: the tutor is able to teach a subject they're most knowledgeable in, while the tutee is able to achieve mastery by working with like-minded peers.


Subjects We Teach:

  • Math
  • Science
  • English
  • History
  • Coding
  • Chess
  • SAT/ACT

With over 50 tutors and students, Silverline Tutoring is a thriving and collaborative community sympathetic to any and all student-needs. Become a student or tutor at Silverline Tutoring by joining our Discord.
